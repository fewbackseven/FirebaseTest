About us

Vision

Spinning Tree Theatre Company has born with the vision of finding new pathways of making theatre through research and training with actors. we put an emphasis on training and process more than its immediate results. Through this creative laboratory woven between different theatre and dance techniques, we search for a way to take action, we search for a way to stand still – we search for a language of the body that surfs between fragments of cultures, digging deep into our collective memories.

About Spinning Tree Theater

Spinning Tree Theatre is an independent theatre company for contemporary artists. Founded in 2015 by actor, director Shakeel Ahmmad. This company was born after a period of research on theatre and dance forms from different cultures. In the past several years, many young practitioners have joined the company and are contributing to this research.

Mission

Bringing the theatre practitioners and artists from different cultures and communities together. Making the theatre based on Interculturalism. Creating the performances by actor’s thinking body and moving mind.
